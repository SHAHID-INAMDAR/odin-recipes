Biryani

Description

Today we are going to make biryani ,the love of life for the non-veg people. Biryani are of different types for example chicken biryani, mutton biryani , dum biryani ets , but here we will be making the most common and most loved, chicken Biryani.

it is spicey hot and delicious in with lots of different spices

Ingredients

  • rice
  • chicken
  • curd>
  • clove>
  • ginger garlic paste
  • garam masala
  • oil
  • chilly powder
  • turmuric
  • corriender powder
  • onion
  • green chilli
  • lemon
  • bay leaf
  • hot spices

steps

  1. clean the cheicken
  2. aplly ginger garlic paste, curd, garma masala powder , lemon , turmuric, salt, chilli powder to the chicken
  3. mix the ingridient well and then leave it for 1 hour for marination
  4. while the chicken is being marinated take some oil in a pan and add chopped onions to it
  5. cook the onion til it turns golden
  6. take out the onion and let it dry
  7. take some rice in a container and wash it twice
  8. after washing soak the rice for 1 hour
  9. take another container add water and salt to it and boil it it will be the water in which we cook our rice
  10. take oil in a kadai and add bey leaf, and all the hot spices
  11. add the marinated chicken in it and cook it for few min
  12. add rice to the container in which we took water to boil
  13. cook the rice about 80 %
  14. drain the water after and separate the rice out from it
  15. take the same container and add a layer of the cooked rice
  16. then add a layer of the cooked chicken and its gravey
  17. repeat the layering process
  18. garnish the top most layer of rice with corriender and the golden onion that we fried earlier

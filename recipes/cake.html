CAKE

description

in this webpage we are going to make a cake ,in particularly an ice cake

ingridients

  • 1 pakacge of chocolate mix cake
  • 1 cup of water
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 galon of chocolate ice cream

steps

  1. prehaet the oven to 350 degree F brease a 9x13-inch baking dish
  2. combine chocolate cake mix ,water,egg,and oil in a large bowl
  3. mix it well with an electric mixer
  4. puor the batter in the prepared baking dish
  5. bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean ,30 min
  6. allow it to cool on a wired track
  7. meanwhile ,remove the caton paper from the ice cream
  8. Use a piece of string or dental floss to cut ice cream in half lengthwise, then place the 2 pieces side-by-side on a sheet of waxed paper
  9. remove the cooled cake from the baking dish and then place it over the ice slab
  10. Place a board or serving platter over cake. Holding onto the waxed paper and board, flip ice cream cake over. Remove the waxed paper and smooth the seam between the ice cream slabs.
  11. cover top with waxedd paper and freeze until very firm . decorate the desert
